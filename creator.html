THE CREATOR!?!

The creator will be the tool with which you write your adventure books. The possibilities are endless as you start writing and linking different sections of the adventure. Write away and never worry that you will lose your place as the easy to understand path diagram will show you exactly which pages connect to one another. Write in any style or any genre, we have provided the tools all you have to do is have fun.
